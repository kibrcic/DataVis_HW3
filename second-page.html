About Me

Hi, I'm from Argentina, a country known for its passion for sports, beautiful landscapes, and rich culture. Growing up in Argentina, I developed a deep love for sports, especially field hockey.

Field hockey has played a pivotal role in shaping my life since childhood. From my early years, the sport became more than just a game for me—it was an avenue for learning, growth, and joy. Whether I’m training hard, competing in matches, or simply playing for the love of it, every moment spent on the field fills me with excitement and purpose.

Field hockey is more than a sport for me—it’s a source of joy, personal development, and community. The life lessons and connections I’ve gained through playing have shaped me into the person I am today, and for that, I am truly grateful.

Why I Love Field Hockey

  • It's a fast-paced and exciting sport.
  • It requires teamwork and strategy.
  • It's a great way to stay fit and active.
  • It has helped me meet amazing people and make lifelong friends.
  • Flexible: It's a well-known international sport and thus teams can be found in most countries
