Welcome to My Website!

Hi, my name is Kiara Brcic, and I'm a 20-year-old student at Northeastern University. I am currently studying Data Science and Business with a minor in Mathematics. One interesting fact about me is that I have a twin brother, who is also studying at Northeastern!

I was born in Argentina, a country known for its passion for sports and vibrant culture. Seven years ago, my family and I moved to Mexico City, where they still live today. Despite the relocation, I stay closely connected with my roots by visiting Argentina at least twice a year. In addition to my twin brother, I also have a younger sister, and our close-knit family has always been a source of support and joy in my life.

Growing up in Argentina, I spent 12 formative years immersed in its culture, which ignited my love for field hockey. This is a sport traditionally played by women in the country, and I started playing at the age of six during primary school, where it was a required activity. Over time, it evolved from being a school obligation to one of my greatest passions. Field hockey has taught me valuable life lessons about discipline, teamwork, and resilience.

This website is where I share my hobbies and interests. Feel free to explore more about my experiences, including my love for field hockey and my academic journey.
