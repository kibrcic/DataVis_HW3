Welcome to My Website!

Hi, my name is Kiara Brcic, and I'm a 20-year-old student at Northeastern University. I am currently studying Data Science and Business with a minor in Mathematics. One interesting fact about me is that I have a twin brother, who is also studying at Northeastern!

Growing up in Argentina and spending 12 years there sparked my passion for field hockey, a sport traditionally played by women in the country. I began playing at the age of six in primary school, where it was a mandatory activity. To learn more about my journey with this sport, please visit the "About Me" section. button.
